Bernie's Organic Attribution by Page & Product

↓ Download PDF

What this is. This maps Wicked Reports' organic revenue to the pages and products that earned it, for May 1 to 31, 2026. It uses Wicked's linear attribution: when several pages take part in one sale, each gets an equal fractional share of the credit, which is why "sales" show decimals instead of whole orders. Section 1 is Wicked's organic total for the month. Sections 2 and 3 break that revenue down by page, and only pages that took part in a sale appear there, so they hold almost all of the revenue but only part of the clicks (see the note under section 1). Read "clicks" in those tables as visits to those pages, not total site traffic. Pages that can't belong to a single product (checkout, account, subscription, order confirmation) are grouped as Checkout / Account.
